Zepto Product Data Analysis (SQL Project)
Problem Statement
This project analyzes Zepto product data to uncover insights related to pricing, discounts, and inventory management. The goal is to identify patterns that can help improve business decisions.

Steps Performed
1. Data Cleaning
Removed records with zero MRP or selling price
Converted prices from paise to rupees
Checked and handled null values
2. Data Exploration
Total number of products
Unique product categories
Stock availability (in-stock vs out-of-stock)
Duplicate product analysis
3. Data Analysis & Insights
Top 10 products with highest discount
High MRP products that are out of stock
Category-wise estimated revenue
Products with low discount but high price
Best value products (price per gram)
Inventory weight distribution by category
